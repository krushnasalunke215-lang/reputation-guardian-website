How Our System Works

Our system helps local businesses monitor, respond to, and protect their online reputation—automatically and securely.

Step 1: Secure Connection

Owner-Controlled Access

The business owner securely connects their Google Business Profile to our system using Google’s official authorization flow (OAuth).

  • We never ask for passwords
  • Access can be revoked anytime
  • Data access is limited to reviews only

Step 2: Continuous Review Monitoring

Our system continuously checks for new customer reviews posted on the business’s Google profile.

  • New reviews are detected automatically
  • Existing reviews are never modified or deleted
  • All monitoring follows Google’s API policies

Step 3: Intelligent Review Analysis

Each incoming review is analyzed in real time to understand:

  • Star rating (positive / neutral / negative)
  • Customer sentiment & Urgency level
  • Critical reviews are prioritized instantly

Step 4: Instant Owner Alerts

When a negative review is detected, the business owner is notified immediately via Telegram with:

  • Review content & Star Rating
  • Time of posting & Priority level
  • Benefit: React within minutes, not days.

Step 5: AI-Generated Reply Suggestions

Our AI generates a professional, polite reply suggestion tailored to the situation (apologetic, calm, brand-safe).

The owner stays in full control and decides whether to post the reply.

Step 6: Data Privacy & Compliance

We follow a strict privacy-first approach. Only authorized businesses can access their own data.

  • No data is shared with third parties
  • Access tokens are securely stored and refreshed
  • All usage complies with Google Business Profile policies
